Wallos: Open-Source Personal Subscription Tracker

Self-hostable web application designed to empower you in managing your finances with ease. Wallos simplifies the process of tracking expenses and helps you gain better control over your finances.

Features

Main Features Of Wallos

These are the main features of Wallos that makes it unique and different from other platforms.

Subscription Management

Keep track of your recurring subscriptions and payments, ensuring you never miss a due date.

Category Management

Organize your expenses into customizable categories, enabling you to gain insights into your spending habits.

Multi-Currency support

Wallos supports multiple currencies, allowing you to manage your finances in the currency of your choice.

Currency Conversion

Integrates with the Fixer API so you can get exchange rates and see all your subscriptions on your main currency.

Data Privacy

As a self-hosted application, Wallos ensures that your financial data remains private and secure on your own server.

Customization

Tailor Wallos to your needs with customizable categories, currencies, themes and other display options.

Sorting Options

Allowing you to view your subscriptions from different perspectives.

Logo Search

Wallos can search the web for the logo of your subscriptions if you don't have them available for upload.

Mobile View

Wallos on the go with fully responsive layout.

Statistics and Graphs

Another perspective into your spendings. Wallos provides you with statistics and graphs to help you understand your spending habits.

Notifications

Wallos will remind you of your upcoming payments through your favorite notification agents (Email, Telegram, Discord, etc.)

Multi Language Support

15+ languages supported. Wallos is available in multiple languages to help you manage your finances in your preferred language.

How can I install it?

You can install wallos on a baremetal server or easily through docker. We also provide a docker-compose file to make it easier for you to get started.
